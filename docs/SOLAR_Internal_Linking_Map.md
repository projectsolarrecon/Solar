# SOLAR Internal Linking Map

Use this map for Legislative Tracker monthly throughline sections and other SOLAR cross-linking. The goal is to choose 2–4 natural internal links based on the month’s dominant pattern. Do not invent paths. Do not force weak links.

## Global rules

- Use internal links only when they genuinely help the reader understand the current update.
- Prefer 2–4 internal links in a Monthly Throughline section.
- Do not use more than one internal link in a single sentence.
- Do not use “click here.” Use natural anchor text.
- Do not link the same internal page more than once in the same throughline.
- If the best internal path is uncertain, avoid the link or ask for validation.

## Monthly Throughline rule

For Legislative Tracker monthly pages, write a short Monthly Throughline section between At a Glance and Key Developments.

The section should:
- interpret the month’s dominant pattern
- connect the update to SOLAR’s broader policy position
- include 2–4 natural internal links selected from this map
- avoid SEO stuffing
- avoid inventing internal paths
- stay concise: usually 2–3 paragraphs
